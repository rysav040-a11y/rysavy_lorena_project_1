Circus Park was opened back in 2005 by professional clowns and CEOs: Rita Book and Amanda Tori Meating.

Both CEOs grew up exposed to county fairs, state fairs, and the circus which helped to foster their love for the entertainment and joy surrounding these

events. Book and Meating eventually met in clown school, got their degrees, and decided to enter into the amusement park business on their terms.


Their goals were to make an amusement park that has all of the classic county fair rides that they grew up with, but with safer features and more permanent

structures. They also wanted to create an open space for professional clowns and circus fanatics, like themselves, to perform and spread their love for the

spectacle and drama of the circus.



Come one come all, from kids to adults, without harming any animals in the process, to Circus Park!

ROCKET COASTERS!


Arguably one of the best park rides,

this in-the-dark coaster takes guests

around planets and through galaxies!


Has lap bars and item cubbies with a

total capacity of 14 persons per train.



    !Caution!

    *DON'T ride if you*

    - Have a preexisting heart condition

    - Are pregnant

    - Are subject to seizures


    *Recommended for ages 8 and up*

    *Duration of ride is 5 minutes*

    *Dark interior and loud music*
